RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made on July 1, 2026, between Jane Smith ("Landlord") and John Doe ("Tenant") for the property located at 42 Example Street, Melbourne VIC 3000 ("Premises").

1. TERM. The term of this lease shall begin on August 1, 2026 and end on July 31, 2027, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

2. RENT. Tenant shall pay Landlord monthly rent of $2,200, due on the 1st day of each month. Landlord may increase the rent by up to 8% annually upon 60 days written notice to Tenant.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $4,400 prior to move-in. The deposit will be held as a bond and may be withheld in full at Landlord's sole discretion for any damage, cleaning, or unpaid rent, without itemized documentation required.

4. LATE FEES AND PENALTIES. If rent is not received within 3 days of the due date, Tenant shall pay a late penalty of $150 plus $25 per additional day. Repeated late payment may result in immediate termination of this lease.

5. TERMINATION. Landlord may terminate this Agreement with 14 days notice for any breach of this lease, including noise complaints, unauthorized guests, or failure to maintain the Premises. Tenant may not terminate early except in cases of Landlord's material breach, and must provide 60 days notice with a termination fee equal to two months' rent.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS. Tenant is responsible for all maintenance and repairs to the Premises, including structural and plumbing issues, regardless of cause, up to a limit of $500 per incident. Landlord is not obligated to make repairs unless the issue poses an immediate safety hazard.

7. SUBLETTING AND ASSIGNMENT. Tenant may not sublet, assign, or allow any other person to occupy the Premises without prior written consent from Landlord, which may be withheld for any reason.

8. PETS. No pets are allowed on the Premises without written permission from Landlord. Violation of this clause is grounds for immediate termination and forfeiture of the security deposit.

9. ENTRY BY LANDLORD. Landlord reserves the right to enter the Premises at any time without prior notice for inspection, repairs, or showing the property to prospective tenants or buyers.

10. UTILITIES. Tenant shall be responsible for all utilities including electricity, gas, water, and internet. Failure to maintain utility payments constitutes a breach of this Agreement.

11. INSURANCE. Tenant is strongly encouraged to obtain renter's insurance. Landlord shall not be liable for any loss or damage to Tenant's personal property under any circumstances, including Landlord's negligence.

12. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Victoria, Australia. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration at Tenant's expense.

13. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements, whether written or oral.
